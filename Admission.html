College Admission

Admission to various classes will start after declaration of results of the respective qualifying examination. (Minority quota and Management quota)

  1. The application in the prescribed form must be filled in completely and signed by the student and countersigned by his/her parent/ guardian.

  2. Students, seeking admission, off line will have to apply in person with the filled in application form for an interview before the admission panel.

  3. No admission is valid unless and until it is granted under the authority of the Principal and the full fees are received by the college.

  4. All admissions are valid for one academic year only. A student, who is eligible for admission to the next higher class will have to submit a fresh application in the prescribed format.

  5. A student admitted to the college shall pay the full fees for two terms. Once admitted to the college, he/she will be considered duly enrolled for the whole academic year though payment is to be made in 2 terms separately.

  6. For admission to any class, the following certificates and documents must be produced

    1. Original School Leaving Certificate.

    2. Original Mark sheet of S.S.C./I.C.S.E./C.B.S.E Board or any other equivalent qualification.

    3. One Xerox copy of the mark sheet.

    4. A Student passing an examination other than the S.S.C. Examination of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary Education, must obtain an Eligibility Certificate from Bombay Divisional Board.

    5. Students of Foreign Nationality – Application for admission from foreign nationals must be accompanied by a clearance certificate from the Indian High Commission / Embassy and another clearance certificate from their respective country’s High Commission/Embassy in India.

  7. Admissions will be provisional until all the necessary certificates are submitted to the college.
