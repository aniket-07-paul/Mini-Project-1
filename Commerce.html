Welcome to the Commerce Stream



Embark on a Journey of Commerce and Business

Discover the intricacies of commerce, business, and finance with our comprehensive range of programs at NES JUNIOR COLLEGE Our curriculum is tailored to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the dynamic world of commerce.

Subjects Offered:

  1. English

    2. Unleash the power of language and literature. Our English curriculum is designed to enhance your communication skills, analytical thinking, and appreciation for the written word. From classic literature to modern prose, our courses cover a wide spectrum of literary genres.

  2. Hindi

    3. Immerse yourself in the cultural and linguistic richness of Hindi literature. Our Hindi courses not only focus on language proficiency but also delve into the vast treasure trove of Hindi literature, poetry, and contemporary writings.

  3. Organisation of Commerce

    4. Gain insights into the organizational aspects of commerce and business. Our curriculum covers topics such as business organization, business environment, and entrepreneurship, providing a solid foundation for a career in commerce.

  4. Environmental Studies

    5. Gain insights into pressing environmental issues with our Environmental Studies course. Explore the interconnections between business practices and environmental sustainability, promoting informed and responsible commerce.

  5. Banking

    6. Explore the fundamental principles of banking and financial institutions. Our Banking courses cover topics such as banking operations, financial services, and regulatory frameworks, preparing students for roles in the banking sector.

  6. Book-Keeping & Accountancy

    7. Develop a strong foundation in accounting principles and practices. Our courses cover bookkeeping, financial accounting, and auditing, providing essential skills for a career in finance and accounting.

  7. Mathematics

    8. Sharpen your analytical and quantitative skills with our Mathematics courses. From basic arithmetic to advanced statistics, our curriculum prepares students for the mathematical challenges encountered in the business and commerce sectors.

  9. Secretarial Practice

    10. Learn the skills required for effective secretarial and administrative roles. Our Secretarial Practice courses cover office management, communication skills, and administrative procedures, preparing students for positions in corporate settings.

  10. Information Technology

  11. Office Management
